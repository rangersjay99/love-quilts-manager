LOVE QUILTS MANAGER — MASTER CHANGE LIST
Verified July 24, 2026

IMPLEMENTED THROUGH UPDATE 7.8.20

HOME
- At a Glance remains first.
- Calendar is immediately after At a Glance.
- This Year shows Quilts Made This Year and Quilts Distributed This Year.
- By Charity shows Available in Storage, Quilts Requested, and Quilts Needed to be Completed.
- Common summary cards remain tappable for direct actions.

CALENDAR — VERIFIED COLOR RULES
- Past demand met: light green.
- Past demand not met: light red.
- Future month fully covered early: light green.
- Other future months: light yellow, including months with no request yet.
- An uncovered current month remains neutral and has a bold plum border.
- Each charity is grouped inside its month card across all requested sizes.
- Charity demand met/covered: light green charity section.
- Charity still short: light red charity section.
- For past months, a charity is met only when its requested quilts were completed/distributed.
- For current and future months, a charity is met when available inventory covers all remaining demand for that charity in the month.
- A visible color key appears above the Calendar.
- Calendar supports direct add/edit.

REPORTS
- Home and Reports use Available in Storage, Quilts Requested, and Quilts Needed to be Completed.
- Third Reports summary block is full-width on iPhone.
- Alternating report-row shading remains.
- Yearly Statistics includes quilts made, quilts distributed, net inventory change, current inventory, charities served, and lifetime distributed.

INVENTORY AND DISTRIBUTION
- Set Current Count supports exact counts including zero.
- Distribution deducts immediately, including 26 out of 26 resulting in zero.
- Repeat-save protection prevents duplicate deduction.
- Distribution correction restores or deducts only the difference.
- Linked distribution transactions are protected.

SYNC
- Sync Now remains visible in the banner and Settings.
- Firebase syncing is retained.

VERSIONING
