LOVE QUILTS MANAGER — MASTER CHANGE LIST
Verified July 24, 2026

IMPLEMENTED IN UPDATE 7.8.18

HOME
- At a Glance remains first.
- Calendar is first immediately after At a Glance.
- This Year shows Quilts Made This Year and Quilts Distributed This Year.
- By Charity shows Available in Storage, Quilts Requested, and Quilts Needed to be Completed.
- Summary cards remain tappable for details/actions.
- Add Quilts remains available directly from the storage summary.

CALENDAR
- Past demand met: light green.
- Past demand not met: light red.
- Future request covered early: light green.
- Other future months use normal covered/partial/shortage status.
- Current month has a bold border.
- Calendar supports direct add/edit.

REPORTS
- Home and Reports use the same wording:
  Available in Storage
  Quilts Requested
  Quilts Needed to be Completed
- Third Reports summary block is full-width on iPhone so wording is not cut off.
- Alternating report-row shading remains.
- Yearly Statistics includes quilts made, quilts distributed, net inventory change, current inventory, charities served, and lifetime distributed.
- Compact and Full PDFs use the same verified wording.

INVENTORY AND DISTRIBUTION
- Set Current Count supports an exact count including zero.
- Distribution deducts immediately, including 26 out of 26 resulting in zero.
- Repeat-save protection prevents duplicate deduction.
- Distribution correction restores/deducts only the difference.
- Linked distribution transactions are protected.

SYNC
- Sync Now remains visible in the banner and Settings.
- Firebase structure and approved-device syncing are retained.

VERSIONING
- Continue with 7.8.x updates.
- Hold Version 8 for a later major release.

FUTURE / ROADMAP — NOT PART OF 7.8.18
