Love Quilts Manager Update 7.7.4
Released: July 20, 2026

Adds distributed-needs reporting.

- On-screen report includes a Distributed Needs table.
- Full print and full PDF include every distribution record.
- Compact print and compact PDF include recent distribution records.
- Each record shows sent date, month needed, charity, size, need, sent, remaining, and completion status where space allows.
- Firebase paths, local-storage keys, authentication, and synchronization flow remain unchanged.
