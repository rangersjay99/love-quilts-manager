Love Quilts Manager Update 7.7.8
Released: July 22, 2026

Direct card editing and report alignment correction.

- Edit now changes the month, charity, size, note, and quantity directly in their displayed Planned Needs card.
- Update Distribution edits sent quantity and date in the same displayed card.
- Save, Cancel, and the smaller confirmed Delete button remain attached to that card.
- Report numeric columns are now On Hand, Requested Needs, and Difference.
- Detail rows, charity totals, and Grand Total use the same column alignment.
- Compact PDF and Full PDF use the corrected order.
- No authentication, Firebase sync, or stored-data format changes.
