Love Quilts Manager Update 7.7.2
Released: July 20, 2026

Need fulfillment and distribution tracking:
- Planned needs can record Quantity Distributed and Date Distributed.
- Completed needs show DISTRIBUTED with Need, Sent, and Remaining totals.
- Partially distributed needs show Sent and Remaining quantities.
- Unfinished needs in past months show PAST DUE instead of Covered.
- Completed needs no longer reserve current inventory for later months.
- Optional checkbox creates the matching Inventory Out transaction for newly distributed quilts.
- Leave the checkbox off when the Inventory Out transaction was already entered.

Retains direct quantity entry, live Firebase synchronization, backups, reports, calendar, and existing data.
Firebase production path retained: organizations/faithful-circle-love-quilts
