VERIFY THE CALENDAR

- Past demand met is light green.
- Past demand not met is light red.
- A future month fully covered early is light green.
- Other future months are light yellow, including months with no request.
- The current month has a bold plum border and stays neutral unless fully covered.
- A charity section is light green when that charity is met/covered.
- A charity section is light red when that charity is still short.
- The expanded color key appears above the Calendar.

Existing data and Firebase synchronization are retained.
