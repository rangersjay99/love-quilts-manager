VERIFY THE CALENDAR

- Past demand met is light green.
- Past demand not met is light red.
- A future month fully covered early is light green.
- A future month that is partial or short remains neutral rather than gold or red.
- The current month has a bold plum border.
- The color key appears above the Calendar.
