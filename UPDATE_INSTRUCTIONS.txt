LOVE QUILTS MANAGER — UPDATE 7.8.17
Yearly Statistics, Home Charity Summary, and Calendar Completion History

BEFORE UPDATING
1. Confirm the current device shows all changes synced.
2. Export a current backup from Settings.
3. Keep a ZIP backup of the current GitHub repository.

UPLOAD
1. Extract this ZIP.
2. Upload every extracted file to the main/root folder of the GitHub repository.
3. Replace files with the same names.
4. Do not delete or replace the firebase-test folder.
5. Wait for GitHub Pages to publish.
6. Close and reopen the app on each device. Refresh once if the old version remains.

VERIFY
1. Confirm the splash and About section show Update 7.8.17.
2. Tap Sync Now and wait for “Shared data verified · All changes synced.”
3. On Home, confirm This Year and By Charity appear below At a Glance.
4. Open Reports and confirm Yearly Statistics appears and the year can be changed.
5. Confirm the third Reports total label wraps fully on iPhone.
6. Confirm the current calendar month has a bold border.
7. Confirm a completed past month is green and an unmet past month is red.
8. Test one small change and confirm it appears on the second approved device.

IMPORTANT
- Quilts Made uses Love Quilts In entries; inventory restorations from distribution corrections are excluded.
- Quilts Distributed uses Love Quilts Out entries.
- Current Inventory is today’s balance even when viewing an earlier statistics year.
- This update does not clear or reinitialize shared data.
- All Set Current Count and linked-distribution safeguards from 7.8.16 remain active.
